About Us

We provide great services and ideass

Mathematics Society is a non-registered, non-profit organization within the department of Mathematics. The members of the society include the undergraduate and postgraduate students, faculty, research scholars of the Mathematics department. The society has been quite active in the past in providing its members, particularly the student members, a platform to showcase and sharpen their talents through a variety of activities planned throughout the year.
